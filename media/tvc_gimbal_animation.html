Two-Axis TVC Gimbal Animation

Concept animation for the Rev A benchtop thrust-vector-control stand. Yaw rotates the outer frame and carries the pitch subsystem; pitch rotates the nozzle carrier; the purple vector shows the commanded thrust direction that would generate vehicle control torque.

The animation is a kinematic visualization, not a finite-element model. Its purpose is to show why yaw inertia is larger: the yaw actuator moves the full pitch/nozzle subsystem, while the pitch actuator moves the smaller inner carrier. That difference is why the pre-hardware model predicts slower yaw response for the same useful servo torque.
